## About This Book

*Disrobed* is my strategic analysis of one of the most important battlegrounds in American politics: the federal judiciary. For decades, the Left has used the courts to advance an agenda that it could never achieve through the democratic process. This book exposes that strategy and provides a detailed plan for conservatives to fight back.

## Key Arguments

I examine how liberal judicial activism has distorted the Constitution's original meaning and undermined democratic self-governance. The book traces the history of the Left's campaign to capture the courts, from the Warren Court era through the modern confirmation battles, and shows how this strategy has transformed the judiciary from an institution that interprets the law into one that makes it.

The book also provides a forward-looking strategy for the conservative legal movement, drawing on the successes of organizations like the Federalist Society and advocating for a more aggressive approach to judicial nominations and constitutional litigation.

## Why It Matters

The battle over the courts shapes every major issue in American life — from gun rights to religious liberty to economic freedom. *Disrobed* provides the intellectual ammunition needed to understand and engage in this fight.
